Overview

Purpose

We're a highly experienced team of diverse individuals who bring exceptional outdoor activities to each and every willing adventurer. For us, it's about giving people from all walks of life the opportunity to experience typically unique activities in a professional, safe and fun environment. We inspire people to overcome their fears and provide unforgettable experiences. Whether you're seeking the best rafting in the country or a family-friendly experience, our expertly crafted excursions cater to adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts alike. Discover some of the most breathtaking rafting locations in Zimbabwe and create lasting memories with our exceptional rafting offerings

Audience

Rafting Travel is a white water rafting company in a city known for its active tourist destinations. Their target market includes men and women ages 18-30 who enjoy being in nature and take pride of their adventurous side .These customers are very knowledgeable about their outdoor experiences, and they expect rafting travel to be knowledgeable too. The company's onboarding process includes training for the team, and they hold monthly meetings to update everyone on the latest developments and recalls in their industry. They post pictures of their employees and customers using their products on their social media page and website.
