Zimbabwe's Bulawayo

Bulawayo is the second largest city in Zimbabwe, and the largest city in the country's Matabeleland region. It's Zimbabwe's principal industrial centre; its major products are automobiles, tires, concrete and other building materials, radios, television sets, textiles, furniture, and food. As the headquarters of Zimbabwe railways, Bulawayo is the country's main transshipment point for goods to and from South Africa
